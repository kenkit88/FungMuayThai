WHO WE ARE

旋風泰拳Fung Muay Thai & Fitness位於葵涌，從葵芳港鐵站步行3分鐘即可到達，位置方便！我們的教練均有豐富比賽及教學經驗，不論你是想修身減肥、增肌健身、放鬆減壓或有意成為拳手出賽，旋風泰拳都可以滿足你嘅要求！

Fung Muay Thai & Fitness is conveniently located in Kwai Chung, just a 3-minute walk from Kwai Fong MTR station! Our coaches have extensive competition and teaching experience, so whether you are looking to lose weight, gain muscle, relax and de-stress, or want to become a fighter, Fung Muay Thai can meet your needs!
